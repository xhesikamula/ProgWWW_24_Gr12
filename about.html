More Info About Us

We are passionate about baking the most delightful pastries!

We are a proud Albanian team, operating from Pastry Lane, Sweetville, but we never forget our roots. Although we are far from home, we strive to bring a piece of Albania to the world. Our love for our homeland has inspired us to include traditional Albanian sweets in our offerings.

One of our favorites is the delicious Tespishte and Bakllava, both made with love and passion, offering a true taste of Albanian culture. These sweets are not just a treat but a reminder of the warmth and hospitality of our homeland.

Siç themi ne shqiptarët: Tespishte për një kafshatë delikate, dhe bakllava për një festë të ëmbël—dy embëlsira tradicionale shqiptare që sjellin gëzim në çdo ngjarje.
